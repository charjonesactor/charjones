About

Char Jones is a versatile actor and dancer based in NYC, with ties to LA and Chicago, known for her dynamic storytelling and cinematic presence. Trained in classical theatre, musical theatre, and devising, she brings energy, depth, humor, and creativity to every project.

Char made her professional debut at age 8 and has since performed in SNL, FBI, Gone Girl, regionally, Off-Broadway, and most recently at La MaMa in NYC and the Prague Shakespeare Company. Her training includes Northwestern University, Juilliard Extension Drama Dept, T. Schreiber Studio, Katie Cappiello, and the Royal Shakespeare Company.

In addition to acting, Char is a trained aerialist, dancer, and diver. She is passionate about new work, classical text, and everything between..
